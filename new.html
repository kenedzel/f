WELCOME TO MCAP 2016!

Eager to transform your ideas into creations? Passionate about designing mobile solutions and disruptive technology addressing different challenges. Join MCAP and be part of the largest competition of its kind spread across six continents. MCAP - Mobile Challenge Asia Pacific is looking for innovative mobile solutions and ideas across the region. At the Regional Finals that brings together entrepreneurs from 10 countries, you can meet and compete for 3 places at the GSMA Barcelona Conference for the Global Mobile Challenge Finals in 2017. This challenge is organized by IE Business School in conjuction with GSMA.

HOW TO REGISTER

  • Do you have a new innovative idea related to mobile?
  • Do you have an early stage start up in the mobile sector?
  • Are you an entrepreneur at heart?

If you answered YES to any of the questions above, then you should register your team to take part in this challenge. Please register your team details only once. We ask that the team leader from every team to enter your mobile solution name to avoid duplicate entries.

ELIGIBILITY

The team must consist of 2-6 applicants

  • At least one applicant from the team has to be between the ages of 18 and 35
  • At least one applicant from the team has to be a resident of a participating country
  • Applicants should not have any current Venture-Capital funding of more than USD $250,000

Eligibility criteria may vary from country to country.

Global Mobile Challenge is entrepreneurship competition where innovators from around the world first compete in their global region to win a trip to Barcelona to take the overall crown. In partnership with 4YFN (Four Years From Now) and Mobile World Congress, The Global Mobile Challenge brings together teams from every continent through regional competitions culminating in a finals in Barcelona.

The biggest mobile application competition for young entrepreneurs & innovators from all over the world featuring apps, IOT and other mobile solutions in both software and hardware. Teams are provided training, education and access to an international network of mentors, angel investors, venture capitalists, and industry leaders.

TIMELINE

The Challenge is conducted in two phases - the Country Semi-Finals and the Regional Finals(held in each of the participating countries) and the MCAP Regional Finals.

Country Semi-Finals

In Phase One, participating teams in each country will have the opportunity to submit their mobile-related applications, software and hardware proposals for consideration. If selected, your team may undergo an Acceleration program. For more details on submission deadlines and criteria, please click on your Country Flag below.

Regional Final

In Phase Two, which is the Regional Final, the three winning teams from each country semi-final will come together to compete. In January 2017 the top three Regional Finalist teams stand to win a trip to Barcelona to showcase their Mobile solution at the at the GSMA Conference.

So why wait? Join now and expand your horizon, explore new ideas, network with developers around the region, gain experience and have lots of fun!

Acceleration Programme

The Acceleration Program is intended to provide competiting teams with a tangible roadmap on how to develop their idea and mobile solution and make them commercial-ready. This involves a one-day bootcamp with mentors, experts, investors and successful entrepreneurs. The bootcamps may not be available in all countries but the MCAP team will be providing online support and mentoring for other teams. Please click on the country flag for updates on acceleration bootcamps in your country.
